Dew Dale Resort at Baratang Island

The island has dense tropical forests ranging from tidal swamp forest to evergreen and littoral forests, with sandy beaches at Baludera and Roglachang.

Mud volcanoes and limestone caves are rare attractions that make Baratang one of the most distinctive travel regions in the Andaman archipelago.

Our Commitment to Responsible Tourism
